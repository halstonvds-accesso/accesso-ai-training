## Module 1: Foundations

### Lesson 0: Course Introduction
**Command:** `/lesson0`
**Time:** 15 min
**You'll learn:**
- What "AI-enabled PM" actually means
- The 4-week idea-to-shipped-feature workflow
- Why speed + validation beats perfection
- What you'll build in this course

**You'll build:** Mental model for the course, initial progress tracking

---

### Lesson 1: Setting Up Your CLAUDE.md & Context Management
**Command:** `/lesson1`
**Time:** 25 min
**You'll learn:**
- What CLAUDE.md is and why it matters
- How to give Claude persistent context about your work
- Keeping CLAUDE.md concise and linking out to detailed docs
- How context management works (CLAUDE.md + lessons/progress.md)
- Making Claude smarter over time

**You'll build:** Personal CLAUDE.md file with your role, products, and preferences

---

### Lesson 2: Workspace Setup & Working with Files
**Command:** `/lesson2`
**Time:** 30 min
**You'll learn:**
- How to talk to Claude naturally (no "prompt engineering" needed)
- How to organize your workspace with folders
- Finding files and content with plain English
- **Extract:** Pull structure from messy documents
- **Synthesize:** Find patterns across multiple files
- **Template:** Control output format consistently
- **Images & Web:** Get insights from screenshots and external info

**You'll build:** Organized folder structure, file processing skills

---

### Lesson 3: Commands & Power User Features
**Command:** `/lesson3`
**Time:** 20 min
**You'll learn:**
- Essential slash commands
- Keyboard shortcuts that save time
- Custom commands and where to find more (accesso.io)
- Building your own commands

**You'll build:** Understanding of commands, optionally your first custom command

---

### Lesson 4: Agents for Parallel Processing
**Command:** `/lesson4`
**Time:** 25 min
**You'll learn:**
- What agents are and when to use them
- Running multiple tasks simultaneously
- The "explore" agent for research
- Processing many similar items at once

**You'll build:** Hands-on experience with parallel research

---

### Lesson 5: Custom Sub-agents & Perspectives
**Command:** `/lesson5`
**Time:** 25 min
**You'll learn:**
- Getting diverse perspectives on decisions (technical, user, business)
- Pressure-testing ideas before real validation
- Pre-meeting prep and PRD review patterns
- **Important:** Perspectives prepare you for conversations, they don't replace them

**You'll build:** Decision-making framework with multiple perspectives

---

## Module 2: Integration

### Lesson 6: Atlassian MCP Setup & Usage
**Command:** `/lesson6`
**Time:** 30 min
**You'll learn:**
- What MCPs are and how to find/install them (just ask!)
- Setting up the Atlassian MCP (Jira + Confluence)
- Configuring your default project and space
- **Guardrails:** Push-only workflow, always ask before syncing
- Basic usage patterns for Jira and Confluence

**You'll build:** Connected Atlassian integration with proper guardrails

**Note:** Optional MCPs (Figma, etc.) can be added the same way - just ask!

---

## Module 3: Practical Application

### Lesson 7: Practical Workflows
**Command:** `/lesson7`
**Time:** 30-45 min
**You'll learn:**
- Workflows tailored to YOUR role (based on what we know about you)
- Options include:
  - Meeting notes & transcription
  - User story writing
  - PRDs & documentation
  - Competitive research
  - Customer interview prep & synthesis
  - API documentation reading
  - Feedback analysis
  - And more based on your needs

**You'll build:** 2-3 practical workflows you'll actually use

**Dynamic:** This lesson reads your progress file and suggests workflows based on your role, responsibilities, and what you've said you want to speed up.
